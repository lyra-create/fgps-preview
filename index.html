The Cost of Navigation Errors

Seismic Navigation Is Too Important
to Leave to Chance

A single positioning error cascades through the entire processing chain. The tools you use are not a line item — they are risk management.

Processing Delays

Vessel time runs at hundreds of thousands of dollars per day. A navigation error discovered late means downtime, reprocessing, and in the worst case — remobilisation. Every hour of delay burns budget that cannot be recovered.

Compliance Risk

Regulatory bodies and E&P clients demand audit-ready positioning records. Generic tools adapted from hydrographic workflows introduce gaps in traceability. FGPS produces comprehensive, defensible QC reports built for seismic deliverable standards.

Quality Uncertainty

Traditional QC is backward-looking — you find problems after the vessel has moved on. Without real-time quality monitoring, you are relying on hope rather than data. SeisCloudNAV puts live navigation quality in your browser, minutes after acquisition.
