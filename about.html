Our Story

25 Years of Getting
Navigation Right

FGPS was built on a simple premise: seismic surveyors deserve tools made by someone who actually understands their work.

Built by a Surveyor.
Not a Committee.

In 2000, Paul Dowd — a surveyor who had been working in seismic since 1981 — co-founded FGPS to solve a problem he'd lived with for nearly two decades: the tools available for seismic navigation processing weren't good enough.

Having worked with Western Geophysical and Horizon Exploration, Paul had seen every type of survey configuration, every data challenge, every "impossible" dataset. When he sat down to write navigation processing software, he wasn't theorising about what surveyors needed — he was building what he'd wished he'd had.

That philosophy hasn't changed. Every FGPS product has been developed using real data from real surveys. The algorithms aren't textbook implementations — they're battle-tested solutions refined over thousands of surveys across every major contractor fleet.

FGPS has grown deliberately and sustainably. We're not chasing hypergrowth or venture capital. We're building the best navigation processing tools in the world, and we're doing it one survey at a time.

What We Believe

Our Principles

Technical Integrity First

Our software exists so surveyors can do more than "just produce a result". Every calculation is reproducible, every algorithm is defensible, every report is audit-ready. We don't cut corners on accuracy.

Sustainable Growth

We've expanded at a deliberate rate for 25 years. No boom-bust cycles, no panic pivots. Our clients can depend on FGPS being here in 5, 10, 25 years — because we're built to last.
