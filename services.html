First Line Quality Control (FLQC)

Independent verification with a 24-hour turnaround.

FGPS provides a comprehensive FLQC service that verifies navigation computer parameters, data quality, and the integrity of final deliverables (P2 and P1 datasets). Used routinely by seismic contractors, consultancies, and exploration companies to achieve the level of confidence that positioning accuracy demands.

This isn't the contractor marking their own homework. It's independent, expert-level verification from a team that has been doing this for over two decades — and that's all we do.

⏱️

24-Hour Turnaround

Results when you need them, not when it's convenient. Our FLQC team operates around the clock during active surveys.

🔍

Comprehensive Parameter Checks

Not just a cursory review. Full verification of navigation computer parameters, datum transformations, processing parameters, and final coordinate accuracy.

Audit-Ready Reports

Professional reports that satisfy client requirements, regulatory audits, and internal quality management systems.

True Independence

FGPS is not a contractor, not an equipment manufacturer, not owned by anyone in the supply chain. Our QC is independent by design.

Line Quality Control (LQC)

Remote processing service to verify data quality by independently processing the contractor's P2 and comparing with the resultant P1. A lighter-touch alternative to full FLQC when you need verification without the complete parameter audit.

Data Processing

Full P2 to P1 processing service tailored to your requirements. Whether it's a complex multi-vessel survey, a legacy reprocessing project, or a straightforward 2D line — our processing team handles it with the same tools and expertise we build into our software.

Consultancy

Advisory services covering all aspects of navigation and positioning for seismic surveys. From survey specification writing to positioning system evaluation, from datum transformation advice to dispute resolution — our consultants bring 45+ years of hands-on experience.

Training

Comprehensive training for all FGPS software products at a time and location suited to your needs. Courses can be augmented to cover geodesy and the least squares solution of survey networks — turning a software training course into a genuine professional development opportunity.
